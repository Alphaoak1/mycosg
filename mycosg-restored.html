Our Story

Not just a farm.

MycoSG was born from a simple question: why is Singapore — a city obsessed with food innovation — still importing most of its gourmet mushrooms from overseas?

Our model draws on the Mushroom Street Farms playbook from Texas — adapted for Singapore's urban density, tropical climate, and health-conscious community.

Information Memorandum

MycoSG FarmBox Programme — 2025 Edition

1. Structure Overview

The MycoSG FarmBox Programme is structured as a two-contract commercial arrangement. It is not a collective investment scheme, managed fund, or financial product regulated under the Securities and Futures Act (SFA).

Contract 1 — Equipment Sale Agreement: The purchaser acquires full legal title to one or more FarmBox units (modified 40-foot shipping containers, fully fitted for mushroom cultivation). Title transfers upon receipt of full payment. The unit is registered on the purchaser's balance sheet and may be resold, transferred, or repurposed subject to MycoSG's right of first refusal.

Contract 2 — Farm Management Services Agreement (FMSA): MycoSG provides full farm management services including substrate preparation, cultivation, harvesting, sales, and distribution. MycoSG retains 30% of monthly net production revenue as its management fee and bears all operating expenses therefrom. The remaining 70% is paid to the unit owner as a variable output service fee within 15 days of month-end.

6. Key Risk Factors

Yield risk: Actual mushroom production may fall below projections due to contamination, environmental factors, or substrate availability. A single contamination event may eliminate 2–4 weeks of production.

Market price risk: Wholesale and retail mushroom prices may decline if supply increases or demand softens.

Operational risk: HVAC failure, power disruption, or labour shortage may temporarily halt production. MycoSG maintains maintenance reserves but cannot guarantee uninterrupted operation.

Asset value risk: The FarmBox unit is a physical asset that depreciates over its useful life (estimated 8–10 years). Resale value is not guaranteed.

Regulatory risk: Changes to SFA, HSA, or MAS regulations may affect programme structure or product sales.

Frequently Asked Questions

FarmBox Programme · Subscriptions · Wellness

FarmBox Ownership

QIs this an investment product regulated by MAS?
No. This is a commercial equipment purchase paired with a farm management services contract. It is not a collective investment scheme. It is offered under the Small Offers Exemption (Section 272A, SFA) and is not a regulated financial product. We strongly encourage independent legal advice before proceeding.
QCan I visit my FarmBox container?
Yes. All unit owners can schedule visits to their container anytime during operating hours. Multi-unit owners (Tier 2 and above) receive complimentary quarterly farm visit packages. Simply contact your account manager to arrange.
QWhat happens if my container suffers a contamination event?
Contamination risk is the primary operational risk in mushroom farming and is clearly disclosed in the Information Memorandum. Our biosecure container design reduces contamination rates by over 60% vs open-air farms. In the event of a contamination event, affected bags are cleared and restocked at MycoSG's cost, with production resuming within 2–4 weeks. Output fees are not paid during non-production periods.
QCan I sell my FarmBox unit?
Yes. You own the physical asset and may sell or transfer it. MycoSG holds a right of first refusal at fair market value. If MycoSG declines, you may sell to any third party subject to them entering into a new Farm Management Services Agreement with MycoSG.
QAre output fees guaranteed?
No. Output fees are variable and directly tied to production output. The three-scenario model in the Information Memorandum represents modelled estimates, not guarantees. Actual returns depend on yields, market pricing, and operating conditions.

Mushroom Box Subscriptions

QHow fresh are the mushrooms?
Every box is packed within 24 hours of harvest and delivered the same or next day via cold chain. Our containers are located close to delivery hubs, so your mushrooms are never more than 36 hours from fruiting body to your door.
QCan I pause or cancel my subscription?
Yes, with no fees or penalties. Pause for up to 3 months at any time. Cancel anytime with 48 hours notice before your next dispatch. No lock-in, no minimum commitment.
QDo you deliver island-wide in Singapore?
Yes. We deliver to all residential and commercial addresses in Singapore, including HDB, condominiums, and offices. Delivery windows are 9am–1pm or 2pm–6pm on your chosen day.

How to Grow

01Remove the kit from the bag and make a 5cm X-cut on the plastic film face.
02Place in indirect light at room temperature (22–28°C works well in Singapore).
03Mist the cut face 1–2 times daily with a spray bottle. Keep humidity high.
04Pins appear in 5–14 days. Harvest when caps are fully open but before edges curl up.
05After harvest, rest the block for 5–7 days, then repeat for 2–3 more flushes.
